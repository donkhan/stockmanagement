About Rico Auto

Rico Auto is in the Auto Ancillaries sector. The current market capitalisation stands at Rs 878.68 crore.

The company management includes Arvind Kapur - Chairman & M.D & CEO, Arun Kapur - Joint Managing Director, Kanwal Monga - Director, Amarjit Chopra - Director, Ashok Seth - Director, Rakesh Kapur - Director, Satish Sekhri - Director, Vinod Kumar Nagar - Director, Rajeev Kapoor - Director, Upasna Kapur - Addnl.Non Executive Director.

It is listed on the BSE with a BSE Code of 520008 and the NSE with an NSE Code of RICOAUTO.

Its Registered office is at 69 K M Stone, Delhi - Jaipur Highway,,Dharuhera Rewari District,Haryana - 122106.

Their Registrars are M C S Ltd.
